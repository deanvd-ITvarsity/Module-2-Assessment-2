OLD KHAKI


Old Khaki offers a contemporary take on classic and conservative clothing, thus offering subtle but high quality clothing that is made to last. Clothing ranges from casual outfits to camping and hiking clothing including jackets and shoes. Not only is it a eutopia for camping and hiking enthusiats,but also an equipment for all kinds of outdoor activity as well as survival kits and various tools. Old Khaki really embrases an outside lifestyle one that is in touch with nature and its beauty that comes with it.It is truly an extraordinary shopping experience with friendly and helpful staff.
